Leadership

The Architect Behind AKcelerate

15+ years building systems that scale companies from $1M to $50M+.

Built in the Trenches, Not Boardrooms

I didn't learn scaling from textbooks. I learned it by doing—building, breaking, and rebuilding operational systems for companies at every stage from seed to exit.

My career started in the chaos of early-stage startups. I was employee #8 at a SaaS company that went from $500K to $25M ARR in four years. I saw what worked, what broke, and what had to be completely rebuilt at each inflection point.

Then I joined a PE-backed firm as Head of Operations, tasked with scaling five portfolio companies simultaneously. That's where I developed the frameworks I use today—repeatable systems for revenue operations, team structure, and strategic planning that work across industries.

After a decade on the operator side, I realized my real value was in the architecture—designing the infrastructure companies need to scale predictably. So I founded AKcelerate.

Operating Principles

How I Work

Systems Over Strategy Decks

Most consultants give you a beautiful PowerPoint and disappear. I build the actual systems with you—process documentation, team playbooks, operational workflows. Real infrastructure, not just ideas.

Embedded, Not Advisory

I don't work from an office downtown. I embed with your team, sit in your meetings, and understand the real bottlenecks. You can't architect solutions from the outside.

Execution, Not Just Planning

Strategy is worthless without execution. I stay through implementation—training your team, optimizing workflows, and ensuring systems actually work in practice, not just in theory.

Data-Driven Decisions

Gut instinct got you to $1M. Data gets you to $10M. Every recommendation is backed by metrics, benchmarks, and projections. No opinions, only evidence.

Build for Independence

My goal is to make myself obsolete. I'm building systems your team can run without me. No dependency, no recurring fees—just sustainable growth infrastructure.

Selective Partnerships

I work with 3-5 companies at a time. Not because I'm exclusive, but because embedded work requires deep attention. Quality over quantity, always.
